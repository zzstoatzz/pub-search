pub search / labels

pub-search is a search engine for writing that someone — a person or an AI — actually wrote. Some accounts publish pages that look stamped out by a system instead: one per TV episode, per patent filing, per recall notice. Those accounts get a public bulk-generated label and are left out of search results — unless deliberately kept. The call is automated: a pattern check nominates, a model on co/core decides.
